Nepali Food Menu

Dal Bhat

Dal Bhat is a traditional Nepali meal consisting of steamed rice and a lentil soup called dal. It is usually served with a variety of side dishes.

Price: NPR 200

Momo

Momo is a type of Nepali dumpling, filled with ingredients such as minced meat or vegetables. It's typically served with a dipping sauce.

Price: NPR 150

Thukpa

Thukpa is a Nepali noodle soup with vegetables and sometimes meat. It's a hearty and warming dish often enjoyed in the colder regions.

Price: NPR 250

Sel Roti

Sel Roti is a traditional Nepali rice-based doughnut-like snack. It's crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, often enjoyed with tea.

Price: NPR 50

Gundruk

Gundruk is a fermented leafy green vegetable, a staple in Nepali cuisine. It's often used as an ingredient in various dishes.

Price: NPR 100

Yomari

Yomari is a traditional Nepali dessert made from rice flour and filled with sweet fillings like jaggery and sesame seeds. It's steamed and served during festivals.

Price: NPR 80
